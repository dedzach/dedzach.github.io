A little about myself. 

Hi, my name is Zach. I'm a keyboard fanatic, runner, athlete, and dog lover turned web developer, with a passion for problem solving, learning , and finding creative solutions to problems.

I decided to pursue a career in web development after 7+ years in the food and service industry. The combination of mental stress and 70+ hour work weeks, eventually started to take a toll on my personal life and I found myself very little time for my personal interests.

I've always had a knack for taking broken things and fixing them ever since I was young. While doing so, every time I encountered a problem I didn't know how to solve, I would spend hours upon hours researching a solution until it was fixed. I found coding a way to help satisfy that natural craving I had for problem solving. 

I found coding fascinating in the sense that each problem encountered was like a new puzzle. At the same time, there was never one correct solution, and because so, it allowed for creativity. It was similar in sense to what I used to do as a kid, and that was find pieces of broken objects, and combine them in ways to make them work as a new item. That is what drew me to coding.
